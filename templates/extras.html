Rilo: Monkey Joke



This is a 3D Animation video I made, in Blender, as my final project for my CGI
class. I was a little bit ambitious in my idea and I was not able to
render the project, due to not having enough cpu power [I used about 15
Macintosh computers overnight in the Georgia State University Digital
Aquarium].

I ended up making a video of my screen running the rough draft on my
computer. The part at the end, with the green background is what it
would have looked like properly rendered, minus the green background:
I had planned to replace the "green screen" with another video.

I got an "A."
